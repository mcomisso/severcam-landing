Privacy Policy for SeverCam

Effective Date: April 20, 2025

1. No Personal Data Collection

SeverCam does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All functionality — including face tracking, camera recording, and visual effects — is processed entirely on your device.

We do not:

  • Collect personally identifiable information (PII)
  • Access your contacts, location, or microphone
  • Track your usage
  • Send data to external servers

2. Camera and Photo Access

To function properly, SeverCam requires access to your:

  • Camera — to record videos with live face tracking
  • Photo Library (optional) — if you choose to save recorded videos

These permissions are used solely to enable the app's core features. We never access or use your camera or photos without your explicit permission.

3. No Third-Party Analytics or Ads

SeverCam does not include any third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking SDKs.

4. Children's Privacy

SeverCam is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone, including children.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our app. Any updates will be posted on this page with the revised effective date.
